About Bolstering Health

Bolstering Health strengthens North Carolina communities by pairing care with capability — connecting people to housing, recovery, mental health support, healthy living, and financial literacy, then staying with them until stability sticks.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that people thrive when they have stability, education, and support. We focus on long-term sustainability by helping individuals and families develop the skills, habits, and connections needed for lasting independence.

Working alongside partners including The Carter Clinic, PA and B&D Integrated Health Systems, we help clients access critical mental health care, counseling, primary care, substance use recovery services, and wraparound supports.

From housing stability and workforce development to financial literacy and children's educational support, Bolstering Health is committed to restoring family units and breaking cycles of instability across underserved communities in North Carolina.
