North Carolina Nonprofit

Connecting North Carolinians with the care and resources they need to thrive.

Bolstering Health strengthens families and communities by pairing care with capability — connecting people to housing, recovery, mental health support, healthy living, and financial literacy, then staying with them until stability sticks.

Our Approach

Meet people where they are. Remove barriers. Follow through.

We blend warm outreach with practical coordination — helping clients keep appointments, navigate systems, and build the skills they need for long-term independence.

What We Focus On

Our work is organized into five core programs. Visit the Programs page to see details, eligibility, and examples of services.

Housing as Health

Stable housing as a foundation

Transitional housing and supports for foster youth, returning citizens, and families facing housing instability.

Substance Abuse & Mental Health

Care continuity & engagement

Steward & Seed

Financial success & investing mindset

Children’s Program
